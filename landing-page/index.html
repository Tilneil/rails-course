Gizmos?

While gizmo often means an unknown object - like a thingamajig - a gizmo is any device that gets a job done. A phone is a gizmo for talking to people. A stove is a gizmo that cooks food. A hammer is a gizmo that pounds in nails. Gizmos are all man-made: a plant or animal can't be a gizmo. If you want to be an inventor, you too can come up with an original gizmo that people use everyday.


HOW IT WORKS...

  1. Sign up to become a free member to send us an idea for your gizmo
  2. Our team will contact you to draft technical plans to create prototypes.
  3. If you approve of our prototype you can purchase the patent or purchase a share into our portfolio for development, public release and sale of the product. Inventors can receive up to 35% of profits.
